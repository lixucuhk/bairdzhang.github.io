Short Bio

My name is Xu Li, currently 3rd-year PhD student in Systems Engineering & Engineering Management department at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), advised by Prof. Helen Meng. My research interests include speaker recognition, voiceprint anti-spoofing countermeasures, mispronunciation detection and diagnosis, and machine learning algorithms.

Before that, I obtained B.E. in Information Science and Technology at University of Science and Technology of China in 2017.
